Osaka

Osaka is our favorite city to stay in. It serves as a great hub by not being too expensive and is centrally located throughout the country. As Japan's 2nd largest city, there is so much to see and do.

Tokyo

One of my favorite memories from this trip was landing at the airport and going stright to the hotel. We were hungry, it was late and the only place open was the rooftop restaurant. We went up there and got seated, only to realize we had the entire restaurant to ourselves and we had a view for miles over the city. It was amazing!

Nara

Nara was super fun. I had really wanted to go to see the bowing deer and the beautiful temples. We were not disappointed.

Shirahama

Shirahama is a resort town that has beaches with white sand imported from Australia and turqouise waters. The weather is pretty tropical most of the year too.

Uji

Uji is the green tea capital of Japan. If you can think it, they most likely have it with green tea added.
